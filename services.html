Everything an event actually needs.

Our scope is intentionally complete. Pick the entire event from concept through breakdown — or only the day-of management. Either way, every detail is run by the same principal.

The four pillars

Coordination · Design · Media · Vendors.

Every SP&E engagement is built from four pillars. You can engage all four for a full production, or any subset for day-of or project-specific support.

01

Coordination

Event design and logistics, organization, planning, research, comparison of cost and services, orders, follow-ups with vendors, management and operation pre- and during the event, set-up and break-down.

  • Budget template & weekly budget management
  • Vendor estimates, negotiation & list
  • Vendor responsibility checklist
  • Floor plan design
  • Set-up schedule & on-site management
  • Minute-by-minute itinerary distributed to every vendor
  • Day-of management or full project coordination
02

Art & Design

All concepts, ideas, logos, designs, adaptations and creations. Designs for printed materials and vendor-specific format requirements for production.

  • Event concept design — theme, color, narrative
  • Invitations (digital or hard copy) & RSVP
  • Programs, place cards, menus, agendas
  • Decals for dance floors & bars
  • Favor tags, thank-you notes, signage
  • Logo & monogram design
03

Media Production

Photography and video pre-production, production, and post — for everything except the ceremony and reception cameras themselves (provided separately by photo/video vendors).

  • Pre-event photoshoot
  • Sign-in album
  • Entrance video (2 min with audio)
  • Ongoing video (up to 400 photos, no audio)
  • Presentation montage (up to 100 photos with audio)
  • Editing, design and production end-to-end
04

Vendor & Production

Sourcing, ordering, and managing every physical item the event needs — from invitations and signage to centerpieces, lighting, sound, and favors.

  • Printed materials & production runs
  • Centerpieces, décor, step & repeat
  • Lighting & sound coordination
  • Props, swag, favors & giveaways
  • Standees, signs & backdrops
  • Direct vendor payments — no markup laundering
