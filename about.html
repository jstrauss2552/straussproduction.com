The studio behind every moment.

SP&E is a boutique event house based in South Florida. We design, plan, and produce events that feel unmistakably like the people they're for — from intimate ceremonies to multi-day productions for hundreds.

Led by Gina Strauss.

Gina has been producing events across South Florida and beyond since 2012. She is the principal you'll meet on day one — and the same person walking the floor on event night. Every event, every detail, one principal in charge.

That's not how most agencies work. It's why ours does.

  • Travel-friendly — destination weddings & productions welcome
