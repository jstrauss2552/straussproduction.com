Honest answers to the real questions.

Most of these come up on the first call. We figured we'd put them here so you can think them through on your own time.

How far out should I book?

For weddings and Bar/Bat Mitzvahs in South Florida, ideally 12–18 months out — the best venues, photographers, and bands book that far ahead. We've turned around quality events in 7 weeks; we've also been booked three years in advance. The earlier you call, the more options you have.

How much do you charge?

Coordination is $2,850 day-of or $5,850 full project for local events up to 250 guests at a single location. A $500 non-refundable deposit secures your date.

Art design, media production, and vendor/printing costs are separate — they depend on what you actually need. We don't mark up vendor invoices; you pay vendors directly.

Destination events, larger guest counts, and multi-day productions are quoted bespoke.

Do you travel?

Yes. We're based in Miami and Fort Lauderdale but produce events anywhere our clients ask us to go. Travel and lodging are billed at cost.

What does "day-of" actually include?

A full pre-event walkthrough with you, vendor sync calls in the weeks leading up, a minute-by-minute itinerary distributed to every vendor, and on-site direction from set-up through breakdown. It's day-of in name, weeks-of in practice.

What's the difference between SP&E and a venue's in-house coordinator?

The venue's coordinator works for the venue — they run the room. We work for you — we run the event. We manage every vendor, not just the food and the chairs. Most of our clients have both.

Will I work with Gina, or someone on her team?

Gina is the principal on every event. You'll meet her on day one, and she's the one walking the floor on event night. For larger productions, support staff and vendors join — but the principal contact is always her.

Do you have preferred vendors?

We have a deep network in South Florida — caterers, florists, DJs, photographers, videographers, lighting, AV, transportation. We don't take kickbacks, so the recommendation is always based on fit, not commission. You can also bring your own — we'll integrate them.

Can I cancel? Is the deposit refundable?

The agreement can be terminated by written notice at any time. The $500 deposit and any subsequent payments are non-refundable, but they can be applied to a future event within one year of the original event date under a new agreement.

What if something happens — weather, illness, force majeure?

Our agreement contains standard force-majeure language that protects both sides for events beyond either party's control — acts of God, pandemic, natural disaster, labor disputes, government activities, utility interruption, or vendor non-attendance. If something happens, we work through it together.

Do you take photos for your portfolio?

Only with explicit consent — checked off in the agreement. Many of our best events are not publicly shared, and we respect that completely.

How do I start?

The fastest way is the inquiry form, but feel free to call directly at (786) 255-3491 or email gstrauss@straussproduction.com. We answer every inquiry personally, usually within 24 hours.

Didn't see your question?

Ask Gina directly. We answer every inquiry personally, usually within a day.
