What's included

The deliverables, by phase.

Discover & Design

  • Discovery conversation — usually 60–90 minutes
  • Custom concept board: theme, color, narrative
  • Budget template tailored to your event
  • Initial vendor longlist with rate ranges
  • Master timeline working backwards from event day

Build & Deliver

  • Vendor estimates, negotiation & contracts
  • Vendor responsibility checklists by station
  • Floor plan to scale with seating & service paths
  • Set-up schedule with per-vendor arrival times
  • Minute-by-minute itinerary distributed to all
  • On-site direction from set-up to breakdown
  • Post-event vendor wrap-up emails & reconciliation
Payment schedule

Straightforward, written down, in your contract.

Coordination is billed in three milestones. Art design and media are invoiced 100% per service. Vendor and printing costs are paid directly to vendors — we do not mark them up.

01

Deposit

$500 non-refundable. Secures your event date in the SP&E calendar.

02

30 days before event

Balance of the coordination fee invoiced. Final budget & timeline locked.

03

Vendor payments

Paid directly by client to each vendor per their own contracts. We negotiate, you transact.

04

Art & media

Invoiced 100% per item upon agreement. Printing and production costs separate (depend on quantity).

Cancellation

What happens if plans change.

The agreement may be terminated by written notice at any time. All deposits and payments are non-refundable, but they may be applied to a future event within one year of the original event date under a new agreement.

SP&E is not liable for failure to perform due to acts of God, pandemic, natural disaster, labor disputes, government activities, utility interruption, or vendor non-attendance — standard force-majeure language that protects both sides.
